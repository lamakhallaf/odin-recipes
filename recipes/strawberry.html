Strawberry Pie Recipe

This is a super yummy recipe that we'll be making today.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Baked pie crust
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup water

Steps!

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Place some strawberries aside and make a compote out of the rest
  3. Whisk cornstarch and water together in a small bowl. Gradually stir cornstarch mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Pour cooked strawberry mixture over berries in pastry shell.
  5. Before serving, whip cream in a medium bowl until soft peaks form.
  6. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!
